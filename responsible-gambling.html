4. Recognizing Problem Gambling

Problem gambling can manifest in various ways, and it's important to recognize the signs. These may include:

  • Spending more money or time gambling than you can afford or intended.
  • Neglecting responsibilities at work, school, or home due to gambling.
  • Lying about gambling activity or chasing losses.
  • Feeling anxious, irritable, or depressed because of gambling.
  • Borrowing money to gamble or to cover gambling debts.

If you are concerned about your gambling habits or those of someone you know, we urge you to seek help.
